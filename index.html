Biography

Yirui Zhang is currently a PhD candidate at the School of Computing, National University of Singapore, focusing on applications of large language models (LLMs). She received her Bachelor degree in Communication Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in 2024, with distinction and multiple scholarships. Her research interests span knowledge graph, multimodal RAG, and trustworthy AI.
